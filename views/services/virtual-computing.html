Welcome to AS IT Solutions, Virtual computing solutions are innovative desktop computing solutions that shift the traditional PC computing infrastructure to a centralized computing model. Within Virtual computing solution, users replaces PC desktops or laptops with the access client devices, called zero or thin clients, to remotely connect to virtual desktops centrally hosted in the servers at the data center.
